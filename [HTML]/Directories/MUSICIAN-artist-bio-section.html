Aurora Eclipse is an electronic music producer and songwriter based in Los Angeles. With a unique blend of synthwave, ambient, and progressive house, their music creates immersive sonic experiences that transport listeners to otherworldly dimensions.

Since their debut in 2020, Aurora Eclipse has released 5 critically acclaimed albums and performed at major festivals worldwide, including Coachella, Ultra, and Tomorrowland.
