Senior Frontend Engineer

Location: Remote Type: Full-time Experience: Mid-Senior level Department: Engineering
Apply for this Job

About the Role

We are seeking a highly skilled and passionate Senior Frontend Engineer to join our dynamic engineering team. You will be responsible for leading the development of our next-generation web applications, ensuring high performance, scalability, and an exceptional user experience.

This role offers the opportunity to work on challenging projects, collaborate with talented designers and backend engineers, and make a significant impact on our product's evolution.

Responsibilities

  • Lead the design, development, and maintenance of scalable and robust frontend applications using modern JavaScript frameworks (e.g., React, Vue, Angular).
  • Collaborate closely with product managers and UX/UI designers to translate user stories and wireframes into high-quality code.
  • Write clean, well-documented, and testable code.
  • Mentor junior developers and contribute to code reviews.
  • Optimize application for maximum speed and scalability.
  • Troubleshoot and debug complex issues across the frontend stack.

Qualifications

  • 5+ years of professional experience in frontend web development.
  • Strong proficiency in JavaScript, HTML5, and CSS3.
  • Extensive experience with at least one modern JavaScript framework (React preferred).
  • Solid understanding of responsive design principles and cross-browser compatibility.
  • Experience with RESTful APIs and asynchronous request handling.
  • Familiarity with version control systems (Git).
  • Excellent problem-solving skills and attention to detail.

Bonus Points

  • Experience with TypeScript.
  • Familiarity with UI component libraries (e.g., Material-UI, Ant Design).
  • Knowledge of SEO best practices.
  • Contributions to open-source projects.
