Horizontal Beam Divider Reveal

Animated beam effects that reveal section dividers as you scroll - creating dynamic visual separation

First Section

Scroll down to see the beam divider animations reveal. Each divider style features unique visual effects including glowing beams, particle trails, and collision effects. The animations are triggered when the divider enters the viewport, creating smooth and engaging transitions between content sections.

Single Beam Sweep

The single beam sweep effect features a glowing line that sweeps from left to right, leaving a trail of light. The beam head creates a bright glow as it moves across the screen, producing a futuristic scanning effect that separates content sections with style.

Center Expand

The center expand beam starts from a glowing core at the center and expands outward in both directions. The expanding light creates a symmetric, balanced effect that draws attention to the midpoint before revealing the full divider line with a radial burst of energy.

Dual Beam Collision

Two beams race from opposite sides of the screen and collide at the center, creating a dramatic flash of light. This effect simulates energy collision with particles bursting outward at the impact point, making it perfect for highlighting important section transitions.

Particle Trail

The particle trail effect combines a sweeping beam with floating particles that rise from the line. These glowing particles create a magical, ethereal quality as they fade upward, adding depth and dimension to the divider animation for an immersive visual experience.

Wave Pulse

The wave pulse effect sends ripples of energy expanding outward from the divider line. Multiple waves pulse vertically, creating a sonar-like effect that emphasizes the boundary between sections with rhythmic, hypnotic motion that captures attention.
