How It Works

Scroll-controlled video creates an engaging storytelling technique where the video playback is directly tied to the user's scroll position. Instead of traditional autoplay, users control the narrative pace themselves.

This technique is perfect for product reveals, interactive tutorials, storytelling websites, and any content where you want users to feel in control of the experience.

Scroll Synced

Bi-directional

Scroll up to rewind, scroll down to advance the video
