Dynamic Depth

As you scroll, foreground elements subtly shift position, creating a sense of depth and dimensionality. This micro-parallax effect adds polish and sophistication to any design.

The jitter is calculated in real-time based on scroll position, with different elements moving at varying speeds for a layered effect.
