How It Works

The horizontal scroll gallery transforms vertical scrolling into horizontal movement within a sticky container. As you scroll down, the gallery items move horizontally, creating an engaging showcase experience.

This technique is perfect for portfolios, product showcases, and any content where you want to present items in a gallery format while maintaining natural scroll behavior.
