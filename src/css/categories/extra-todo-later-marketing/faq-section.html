3. Categorized FAQ with Tabs

What is JaZeR and how does it work?

JaZeR is an all-in-one platform that helps teams collaborate, manage projects, and automate workflows. It combines project management, communication, and file sharing into a single unified workspace.

Who is JaZeR designed for?

JaZeR is perfect for teams of all sizes - from startups to enterprises. Whether you're in tech, marketing, design, or any other industry, our flexible platform adapts to your workflow.

How long does it take to set up?

Most teams are up and running within 15 minutes. Our guided onboarding process helps you import existing data and configure your workspace quickly.

What plans are available?

We offer Free, Pro ($12/user/month), and Enterprise plans. Each plan includes different features and support levels to match your team's needs.

Is there a discount for annual billing?

Yes! Annual billing gives you 2 months free (about 17% savings). You can switch between monthly and annual billing at any time.

Can I change plans later?

Absolutely. You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When upgrading, you only pay the prorated difference. Downgrades take effect at the next billing cycle.

Is there an API available?

Yes, we offer a comprehensive REST API with full documentation. Pro and Enterprise plans include API access for custom integrations and automation.

What browsers are supported?

JaZeR works on all modern browsers including Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. We also have desktop apps for Mac and Windows, plus mobile apps for iOS and Android.

How is my data backed up?

All data is automatically backed up daily with point-in-time recovery. Enterprise customers can configure custom backup schedules and export data at any time.

How do I contact support?

You can reach our support team via live chat (available 24/7), email at support@jazer.com, or through our help center. Response times vary by plan.

Do you offer training or onboarding help?

Yes! All customers have access to our knowledge base and video tutorials. Pro customers get group training sessions, and Enterprise customers receive personalized onboarding.

What's the average response time?

Free plan: within 24 hours. Pro plan: within 4 hours. Enterprise plan: within 1 hour with priority queue and dedicated support representative.
